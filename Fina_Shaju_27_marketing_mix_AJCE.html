Introduction

This case study analyzes how Amal Jyothi College of Engineering applies the Marketing Mix (4Ps) – Product, Price, Place, and Promotion – in the higher education sector. The analysis is from a student perspective and focuses on academic offerings, campus facilities, accessibility, and promotional strategies.

About Amal Jyothi College of Engineering

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE) was established in 2001 and is located at Kanjirappally in Kottayam district, Kerala. The college is approved by AICTE and affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU). Managed by the Catholic Diocese of Kanjirappally, AJCE is a NAAC ‘A’ Grade institution with several NBA-accredited programs.

  • Offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional courses in engineering, management, and computer applications
  • Green campus with modern infrastructure, well-equipped labs, and a central library
  • Strong focus on research, innovation, and industry collaboration
  • Active placement cell with partnerships with top recruiters
  • Cultural, technical, and sports activities for holistic student development

AJCE aims to provide quality education with ethical values, preparing students to excel in their careers and contribute positively to society.

1. Product – Academic & Support Services

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering offers programs like B.Tech, M.Tech, BBA, BCA, and MCA with modern curriculum and experienced faculty. The college provides well-equipped laboratories, a digital library, hostels, and sports facilities to support student learning and development. Various student clubs, workshops, and innovation activities help enhance skills and practical knowledge. Mentorship and career guidance are also provided to prepare students for professional success.

3. Place – Location & Accessibility

  • Located in Kanjirappally, Kottayam district, Kerala, surrounded by serene greenery and hills
  • Well-connected by road to major towns and cities like Kottayam (35 km) and Pala (10 km)
  • Regular college bus services and nearby public transport facilities
  • Peaceful learning environment with low pollution and natural surroundings
  • Proximity to research centers, IT parks, and industries for internships and collaborations
  • Safe and student-friendly campus with CCTV surveillance and security personnel
  • On-campus hostels within walking distance to classrooms and labs
  • Accessible healthcare and emergency services nearby
  • Ample parking and green campus promoting sustainable practices
